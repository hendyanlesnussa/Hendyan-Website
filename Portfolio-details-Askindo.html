ASKINDO Project's

ASKINDO (Indonesian Cocoa Association) is an association that was formed to reduce the government's helping hand in handling various kinds of cocoa problems starting from processing producers, traders (including exporters) and cocoa manufacturers internally and integrally. The association is open to all economic actors involved in the field of cocoa in the form of BUMN, private companies, cooperatives and farmer groups.

And this is the third project that I worked on with my section head at Mola TV, Rio Kurniawan. In its development, we used the Avada theme and several Ninja plugins in Wordpress, and this was my first experience using Wordpress in developing a website.
